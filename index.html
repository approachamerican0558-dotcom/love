A Letter From My Heart

My dearest Chanda,


There are words the dictionary hasn't invented yet — words big enough, warm enough, alive enough to describe what you mean to me. Every morning the first thought that dresses my mind is you. Every quiet evening, it is your laugh that fills the silence.


You came into my world like the first light after the longest winter, and since that day, colour has never quite left. You are not just someone I love — you are someone I choose, every single day, without hesitation, without condition.


Thank you for seeing me, fully. For staying when it was hard. For laughing with me until the walls shook. For whispering courage into my doubts. You are my favourite story, and I pray it never ends.


With every beat of this heart —

Forever Yours, Rishi ♡
